About Pune University

Pune University, established in 1949, is one of India's premier institutions for higher education. Our MBA program is designed to equip students with the skills needed to excel in today's dynamic business environment.

With a legacy of excellence spanning over seven decades, we offer a transformative learning experience through our distinguished faculty, industry collaborations, and state-of-the-art infrastructure.
